Public Speaking

I am available for speaking engagements on topics including sport, research, dual careers, and inclusion. Reach out if you're interested in having me speak at your event, workshop, or panel.

My experience includes the insurance, medical, and telecommunications fields. Notable clients are Allianz, Axa, and the Hartmann group. In addition, I had speaker appointments for various charitable organisations and schools.

The talks are tailored to the audience, ranging from small group sessions to large panel discussions, and can be delivered in English or German. I am also open to virtual events. If you'd like more information, please reach out directly to me!
